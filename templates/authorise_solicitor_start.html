You can authorise your solicitor or conveyancer to update the land registry on your behalf when buying or selling a property.

You will need to

  • have chosen and instructed your solicitor or conveyancer
  • be able to identify yourself using GOV.UK
  • confirm what you want your solicitor or conveyancer to do
