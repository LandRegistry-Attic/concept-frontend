Land registry Authorise a solicitor or conveyancer Concept

If you wish to buy or sell a property, or change details on the register, you will need to authorise a solicitor or conveyancer to act on your behalf with the Land Registry.

To authorise a solicitor or conveyanver you will need to

  • have chosen and instructed your solicitor or conveyancer
  • verify your identity on GOV.UK
  • confirm the property details
  • confirm your solicitor or conveyancer's details
